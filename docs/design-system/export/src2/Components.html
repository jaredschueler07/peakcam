01 · Color

Earth + snow. Three families — cream paper, forest green, bark brown — with cold sky-grey for data surfaces. Alpenglow persimmon is the only hot accent; use it for primary CTAs, live indicators, and one element per screen. Mustard is reserved for the GOOD condition state.

04 · Shadows, radii & surfaces

The stamp is the signature. One hard-offset shadow pins a control to the paper; the long stamp is reserved for hero cards and stickers. Soft shadow only appears on hover states or modal lifts.
